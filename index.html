Company Profile / 会社概要

A Japan-based land operator and tourism consultancy bridging local experience with global demand.

Founded in Wakayama Prefecture, Kanko Bridge specializes in travel service arrangement, inbound tourism planning, and regional development consulting — with deep roots in the Kumano region of the Kii Peninsula.
